# Trademark and Branding Policy

The names "QuestPilot", "LightZirconite", "Microsoft Rewards Bot", "Core", and
the project's logos, banners, badges, screenshots, visual identity, and related
branding are reserved by the project maintainers.

You may use the project name only to make truthful, non-misleading references,
such as saying that your contribution is intended for the official Microsoft
Rewards Bot repository.

You may not:

- present a fork, copy, mirror, package, installer, binary, Discord server,
  website, or service as official unless it is controlled by the maintainers;
- imply endorsement, affiliation, partnership, or ownership without written
  permission;
- use project branding to promote a competing repository, service, paid build,
  unlocked Core replacement, or unofficial distribution;
- remove attribution or hide that your work is based on the official project;
- use confusing names such as "official", "Core unlocked", "premium free",
  "QuestPilot V2", or similar wording that suggests official status.

Unofficial forks or patches must clearly state that they are unofficial and must
link back to the official repository.
